Acrylic Oil Paintings

The inspiration for the series of paintings were the beautiful photos of Kathleen T.Carr, which I found in the catalog Walks in the Neighborhood. The works are made with the technique of acrylic base and oil details. They were all made using the recycling method, on canvases I gave a second life.
